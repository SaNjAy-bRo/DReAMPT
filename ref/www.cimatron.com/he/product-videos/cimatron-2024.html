Dimensions and Geometric Datum and Tolerancing (GD&T) - Cimatron 2024 Highlights

Cimatron 2024 introduces new minimum distance dimensioning, enhanced symbols, and improved geometric datum and tolerancing (GD&T)

Find out more here : https://www.cimatron.com/en/whats-new

Mold Runners - Cimatron 2024 Highlights

Within mold design, the construction of 3D runners has been improved to provide control of the design based upon constant volume of vertical orientation and new section orientation control

Find out more here : https://www.cimatron.com/en/whats-new

#Cimatron #manufacturing #moldmaking #toolmaking #engineering #cncmachining #innovation #cadcam

Mold Work Coordinate System (WCS) - Cimatron 2024 Highlights

Any change to the Work Coordinate System (WCS) in a part can now be applied and previewed at the assembly level. This will show the effect of the change in "Mold Position". The benefit for the user are:

Fewer edits to find the right mold direction of draw.

Exactly accurate positioning in Mold Z or rotation, without having to measure and copy/paste numbers that may be rounded off.

Find out more here : https://www.cimatron.com/en/whats-new

#Cimatron #manufacturing #moldmaking #toolmaking #engineering #cncmachining #innovation #cadcam

Automatic Rib Design - Cimatron 2024 Highlights

CAD enhancements include expanded rib construction with additional functionality for working on multiple curves in a single operation, auto-extending rib geometry to part side walls, and the ability to create partial results in complex scenarios where a complete rib cannot be produced

Find out more here : https://www.cimatron.com/en/whats-new

#Cimatron #manufacturing #moldmaking #toolmaking #engineering #cncmachining #innovation #cadcam

Electrode Burn Body - Cimatron 2024 Highlights

Electrode design, a critical aspect of mold production has also been enhanced in Cimatron 2024. The software now includes automation for the Burn Body operation to optimize electrode body shape, control electrode extensions, and set non-cutting rules for manual construction.

Find out more here : https://www.cimatron.com/en/whats-new

Chain selection for chamfer faces - Cimatron 2024 Highlights

Cimatron 2024 also introduces a clean new GUI for controlling drafting standards, improved draft analysis visualization, and automated chain selection of chamfer faces.

Find out more here : https://www.cimatron.com/en/whats-new

#Cimatron #manufacturing #moldmaking #toolmaking #engineering #cncmachining #innovation #cadcam

3D Drawbead construction - Cimatron 2024 Highlights

Die designers can now easily build 3D drawbead geometry to control material flow during the drawing operation in order to achieve the optimal forming of a part without cracks and wrinkles. The new routine will generate the drawbead based upon section type and automatically blend the result into the binder faces.

Find out more here : https://www.cimatron.com/en/whats-new

#Cimatron #manufacturing #moldmaking #toolmaking #engineering #cncmachining #innovation #cadcam

Automatic Feed Control (AFC) - Cimatron 2024 Highlights

The Enhanced Automatic Feed Control (AFC) routine has been completely redeveloped, resulting in smoother and safer motions, faster machining time, reduced sudden changes on machine spindles and axes, and longer tool life. Live benchmark testing with Sandvik Coromant has demonstrated cutting time reductions exceeding 10% on CNC machines

Find out more here : https://www.cimatron.com/en/whats-new

NC Divide Procedure - Cimatron 2024 Highlights

Cimatron 2024 includes the ability to split toolpath by tool life or cutting length, providing greater control and optimization during machining operations.

Find out more here : https://www.cimatron.com/en/whats-new

Pre Drilling Inside Roughing Operations - Cimatron 2024 Highlights

A new option for pre-drilling within roughing operations has been introduced to prevent cutters from plunging into blind pockets. Cimatron will automatically define the optimum position and depth for pre-drilling and the routines will be part of the roughing procedure

Find out more here : https://www.cimatron.com/en/whats-new

5-Axis Auto Tilting - Cimatron 2024 Highlights

5-axis auto tilting has undergone significant improvements, resulting in calculation time gains of over 25 times in certain cases.

Find out more here : https://www.cimatron.com/en/whats-new

3-Axis Deburring - Cimatron 2024 Highlights

Cimatron 2024 introduces a new 3-axis deburring procedure to create chamfers or fillet shapes along sharp edges. Users can generate constant width or constant depth chamfers, or constant width or constant radius fillets.

Find out more here : https://www.cimatron.com/en/whats-new

On Machine Inspection / NC Probing - Cimatron 2024 Highlights

The On Machine Inspection Probing module now supports multipoint selection and cylindrical-shaped probes, enabling more versatile and precise inspection processes

Find out more here : https://www.cimatron.com/en/whats-new

Toolpath Simulation Speed Improvments - Cimatron 2024 Highlights

Within Cimatron 2024 the toolpath simulator calculation time has been significantly improved, resulting in an average productivity gain of over 30%.

Find out more here : https://www.cimatron.com/en/whats-new

Edit Single Drilling Sequence - Cimatron 2024 Highlights

New in Cimatron 2024 the user can edit a drill sequence externally. When the edit is complete the user can overwrite the original, or create an entirely new sequence. This is a great new tool that will speed up sequence editing

Find out more here : https://www.cimatron.com/en/whats-new

#Cimatron #manufacturing #moldmaking #toolmaking #engineering #cncmachining #innovation #cadcam

NC Template Manager - Cimatron 2024 Highlights

NC Templates are used to Automate the NC process. Typically, users have many templates and require a tool for managing them. The new Template Manager helps users managing their templates by:

Managing templates in file explorer, copying, moving, and deleting them.

Showing the procedures within each template and showing important parameters within each procedure.

Edit parameters of each procedure.

Find out more here : https://www.cimatron.com/en/whats-new

#Cimatron #manufacturing #moldmaking #toolmaking #engineering #cncmachining #innovation #cadcam

CoroPlus® Tool Library - Cimatron 2024 Highlights

Cimatron 2024 unlocks the power of Sandvik Coromant by connecting directly to the CoroPlus® Tool Library which provides access to 900,000+ cutting tool items and makes tool recommendations based on material, operation, and tool type, optimizing the machining process and enabling better results without manually making data entries

Find out more here : https://www.cimatron.com/en/whats-new

#Cimatron #manufacturing #moldmaking #toolmaking #engineering #cncmachining #innovation #cadcam

TDM Tool Management System - Cimatron 2024 Highlights

Cimatron 2024 places a strong emphasis on digital connectivity, and the latest release connecting directly with the TDM tool management system. This integration optimizes the machining process, delivering better results without the need for manual data entry

Find out more here : https://www.cimatron.com/en/whats-new
