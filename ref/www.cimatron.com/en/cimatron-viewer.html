Simplified collaboration

Quicky and reliably import files to analyze and validate designs, drawings, parts, assemblies, and manufacturing data, and visualize engineering changes. Comprehensive CAD/CAM information is accessed using a simplified and intuitive interface that enables project managers to check incoming data for quotation and estimation information.

view_in_ar

Mold design review

Mold design review helps verify the performance of major design features — including runners, gates, cooling and venting systems, and more. Material properties, the location of parting lines, undercuts, and parting methods are validated, as well as clearance between mold features and components like ejector pins and baffles. Design verification also calculates factors such as mold type and weight, tonnage requirements, cavitation, and more.

token

Die design review

Comprehensive design reviews make it easy to analyze factors such as material properties, yield, and tensile strength, as well as material thickness, optimal tonnage, and feasibility. Detailed reviews account for everything from dimensions and feeds to process planning, coil width, and stripper pads. Operations such as drawing, forming, trimming, and piercing can be displayed in different colors for straightforward interpretation.

fact_check

Tool build review

Assembly is easy to visualize with tool build reviews that enable zooming, panning, rotating, dynamic sectioning, and viewing components as wireframe models. Tools that allow users to hide or show various plates and components, isolate layers, graphically dismantle assemblies, and open and close tools help simplify complex build processes. Access to build details makes quick work of reviewing geometric dimensioning and tolerancing (GD&T) and analyzing product manufacturing information (PMI).

library_add_check

CNC machining process review

Detailed reviews of CNC processes help analyze the time required for machining operations and tool changes, as well the configurations of spindles, tool holders, fixtures, and clamps. Comprehensive reviews include machining parameters for 2.5-axis to 5-axis toolpath, as well as 3+2 machining operations. Shop-floor staff have the flexibility to quickly generate G-code as needed for any CNC machine.
